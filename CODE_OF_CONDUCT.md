# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

Project participants must communicate professionally, review ideas on their technical merits, respect differing experience and viewpoints, and accept constructive feedback. Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized language, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, and publication of another person's private information are not permitted in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, limit participation, or ban participants whose behavior violates this policy.

This policy applies in repository discussions, pull requests, issues, reviews, and other spaces where someone represents the project.
